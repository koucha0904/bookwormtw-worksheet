## What's inside this worksheet?

This 6-page Finland worksheet walks your child through Finland in three chapters:

1. **Find Finland on the map** — where is it, and how far from home?
2. **Flag coloring page** — the blue cross stands for lakes and sky, the white for winter snow.
3. **How do the Northern Lights happen?** A simple diagram of solar wind and the magnetic field.
4. **Sauna culture** — Finns have roughly one sauna for every two people!
5. **Santa's hometown** — Rovaniemi sits right on the Arctic Circle.
6. **5 Finnish phrases** — Moi (hi), Kiitos (thanks), and more.

## Why I made this for my daughter

She was eight when we went to Finland. I wanted her to feel like she already knew the country before the plane landed, so I turned my own research into worksheets she could color, sticker, and question. She carried it with her the whole trip — she even pulled it out at Santa's Village to compare notes.

## A little fact — what is sisu?

Sisu is the character trait Finns are most proud of — roughly "grit" or "never-give-up." Next time your child is stuck on something hard, try saying: "Time to bring out your sisu!"
