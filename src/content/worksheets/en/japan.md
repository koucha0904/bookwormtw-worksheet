## What's inside this worksheet?

The 8-page Japan worksheet covers four themes: geography, nature, culture, and language.

1. **Map puzzle**: the four main islands — Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, Kyushu.
2. **Mt. Fuji coloring page**: why is it Japan's symbol?
3. **Four seasons**: spring sakura, summer festivals, autumn maples, winter snow.
4. **Washoku**: the origin stories of miso soup, sushi, and ramen.
5. **Write 5 kanji**: 山, 水, 火, 木, 日 — can your child guess the meaning from the shape?
6. **5 Japanese phrases**: konnichiwa, arigatou, sumimasen, and more.
7. **Bowing etiquette**: light, medium, and deep — a fun physical activity.
8. **My Japan diary**: a blank page for your child to draw their favorite memory after the trip.

## Perfect for family travel

Japan is the first overseas trip for many families. This worksheet prints and staples into a little travel booklet. Before our Kyoto trip, my daughter finished hers and spontaneously greeted a shopkeeper with "sumimasen" — that moment made all the prep worth it.
